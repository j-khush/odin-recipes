Lasagna

Description

A delicious alternative to traditional lasagna. Great for a dinner party, even when entertaining non-vegetarians!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) package instant lasagna noodles
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • 2 large carrots, shredded
  • 2 large zucchini, diced
  • 2 summer squash, diced
  • 1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Lightly grease one 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of tomatoes in the bottom of the baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, spinach, carrots, zucchini, summer squash and broccoli.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over broccoli layers and on top of dish.
  5. Season to taste with oregano, salt and pepper.
  6. Repeat layering of ingredients until all are used up.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes.
